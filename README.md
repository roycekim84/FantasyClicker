# Pixel Mercenary Clicker (v0.3)

A small, local-only medieval fantasy clicker with a pixel-art vibe. Click monsters to earn gold, collect relics and runes, complete quests, and prestige for Honor.

## Run

Open `index.html` in any modern browser. No build step or dependencies.

## v0.3 Features

- Regions with themed enemies and chapter bosses every 10 zones.
- Rune system: buy runes, socket one into your equipped relic.
- Skill loadout: equip Power Strike or Battle Cry.
- Achievements with cosmetic titles.
- Random short events for temporary buffs.
- Offline progress summary on return (capped for safety).

## UI

- One-screen layout with tabs (Shop, Inventory, Runes, Quests, Achievements, Log).
- No page scrolling; long lists scroll inside panels.
- Optional Compact mode toggle in the header.

## How to Play

- Click the monster to deal damage. Auto DPS ticks every 100ms.
- Kill monsters to earn gold and progress zones.
- Every 10 kills is a boss; every 10 zones is a chapter boss.
- Every 25 kills in a zone moves you to the next zone.
- Spend gold in the shop to upgrade damage, auto DPS, and critical hits.

## Saving

- Autosaves every 5 seconds.
- Use the Save button for a manual save.
- Reset wipes local storage after confirmation.
